# TSONet Resolution Degradation Reproduction Kit

This repository contains the reproduction kit for evaluating the robustness
of TSONet under simulated spatial resolution degradation of PhiSat-2 imagery.

The original 4.75 m/pixel imagery is compared against simulated resolutions
of 10 m/pixel, 15 m/pixel, and 30 m/pixel. The 10 m and 30 m settings follow
the sensor-information experiment reported in the original TSONet paper,
while 15 m is added as an intermediate experimental condition.
